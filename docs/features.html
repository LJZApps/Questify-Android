Detailed Features

Questify offers a range of features designed to transform your daily tasks into exciting adventures. Here's a detailed look at what you can do with Questify:

Main and Side Quests

Organize your tasks as epic quests with different levels of importance:

  • Main Quests: Important tasks that represent significant goals or responsibilities
  • Side Quests: Smaller tasks that support your main quests or represent less critical activities
  • Quest Tasks: Break down complex quests into smaller, manageable tasks
  • Difficulty Levels: Set difficulty levels for your quests to earn appropriate rewards
  • Due Dates: Set deadlines for your quests to stay on track
  • Notes: Add detailed notes to your quests for additional context

Points System

Earn points by completing quests and use them for rewards:

  • Points Earning: Gain points based on quest difficulty and completion
  • Points Shop: Exchange points for real-world rewards you define
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor your point accumulation over time
  • Leaderboards: Compare your points with friends for friendly competition

AI-Supported Task Evaluation

Our intelligent AI ensures fair rewards for your quests:

  • Smart Difficulty Assessment: AI helps determine appropriate difficulty levels
  • Fair Point Allocation: Ensures points awarded match the actual effort required
  • Quest Suggestions: Receives recommendations for new quests based on your patterns
  • Optimization Tips: Get advice on how to improve your quest completion rate

Discoverable World Map

Explore a mysterious map that unlocks as you move around:

  • Location-Based Discovery: Unlock new areas as you visit different locations
  • Hidden Treasures: Find special rewards in unexplored areas
  • Movement Tracking: Earn points for physical activity
  • Area Challenges: Complete special quests tied to specific locations

Life Trophies

Collect trophies for important life achievements:

  • Life Milestones: Earn trophies for significant life events (marriage, new job, etc.)
  • Achievement Categories: Trophies are organized into different life categories
  • Custom Trophies: Create your own trophies for personal achievements
  • Trophy Display: Showcase your collection to friends

Friends System

Connect with friends for motivation and competition:

  • Friend Connections: Add friends to your network
  • Stat Comparison: Compare your progress with friends
  • Collaborative Quests: Take on challenges together
  • Motivational Messages: Send encouragement to friends

Health and Life Statistics

Track your health data and life goals:

  • Health Integration: Sync with health apps to track physical activity
  • Activity Points: Earn points for movement and exercise
  • Life Goal Tracking: Monitor progress toward major life objectives
  • Wellness Insights: Get data-driven insights about your health habits

Routines

Create recurring tasks and habits:

  • Routine Creation: Set up regular tasks that repeat on a schedule
  • Habit Building: Track consistency with daily or weekly routines
  • Routine Tasks: Break down routines into individual tasks
  • Streak Tracking: Monitor your consistency with routines

EpicQuest Battleground

Engage in PvP mini-games with other users:

  • Character Building: Develop your avatar with points earned from quests
  • PvP Battles: Compete against other players in exciting challenges
  • Rankings: Climb the leaderboards with your battle performance
  • Daily Challenges: Take on special daily battle tasks
  • Guilds: Join forces with other players in guilds
  • Seasonal Events: Participate in limited-time special events

Customizable User Interface

Personalize your Questify experience:

  • Theme Selection: Choose from different visual themes
  • Layout Options: Customize how information is displayed
  • Notification Preferences: Control when and how you receive alerts
  • Accessibility Features: Adjust the app to meet your accessibility needs
