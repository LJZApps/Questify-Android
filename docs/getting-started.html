Getting Started with Questify

Welcome to Questify! This guide will help you get started with transforming your daily tasks into epic adventures. Follow these steps to begin your journey:

1 Download and Install

First, download Questify from your device's app store:

  • For Android: Google Play Store
  • For iOS: Apple App Store

After downloading, follow the installation instructions for your device.

2 Create Your Account

When you first open Questify, you'll need to create an account:

  1. Tap "Sign Up" on the welcome screen
  2. Enter your email address and create a password
  3. Alternatively, sign up with your Google or Apple account
  4. Complete your profile by adding your name and optional profile picture

Tip:

Use a strong, unique password for your Questify account to keep your data secure.

3 Complete the First-Time Setup

After creating your account, you'll go through a brief setup process:

  1. Choose your preferred theme and interface style
  2. Set your notification preferences
  3. Connect health apps if you want to track physical activity
  4. Take a quick tour of the app's main features

4 Create Your First Quest

Now it's time to create your first quest:

  1. From the dashboard, tap the "+" button
  2. Choose "New Quest"
  3. Enter a title for your quest
  4. Add optional notes to provide context
  5. Set a difficulty level (Easy, Medium, Hard, Epic)
  6. Add a due date if applicable
  7. Tap "Create Quest" to save

Tip:

Start with a few simple quests to get familiar with the system before adding more complex ones.

5 Add Tasks to Your Quest

Break down your quest into manageable tasks:

  1. Open your newly created quest
  2. Tap "Add Task"
  3. Enter a description for the task
  4. Repeat to add more tasks as needed

6 Complete Quests and Earn Points

As you complete tasks and quests:

  1. Check off tasks as you complete them
  2. When all tasks are done, mark the quest as complete
  3. Earn points based on the difficulty of the quest
  4. View your points in the dashboard

7 Set Up Routines

For recurring tasks, create routines:

  1. From the dashboard, tap the "+" button
  2. Choose "New Routine"
  3. Enter a title for your routine
  4. Add tasks to the routine
  5. Set the frequency (daily, weekly, etc.)
  6. Tap "Create Routine" to save

8 Connect with Friends

Make your adventure more social:

  1. Go to the "Friends" tab
  2. Tap "Add Friend"
  3. Search for friends by username or email
  4. Send friend requests
  5. Once connected, you can view each other's progress and achievements

9 Explore the World Map

Discover new areas and earn additional points:

  1. Go to the "Map" tab
  2. Enable location services when prompted
  3. Move around in the real world to unlock new areas on the map
  4. Find hidden treasures and complete location-based challenges

Tip:

The map feature works best when you're out and about, so take Questify with you on your daily journeys!

10 Redeem Points in the Shop

Use your hard-earned points for rewards:

  1. Go to the "Shop" tab
  2. Browse available rewards
  3. Select a reward to redeem
  4. Confirm your selection
  5. Points will be deducted from your balance

Tip:

Set up your own real-world rewards in the shop settings, such as "Watch a movie" or "Buy a special treat."

Need Help?

If you encounter any issues or have questions about using Questify, you can:

  • Check our FAQ section
  • Contact our support team at support@questify.app
  • Visit our community forum to connect with other users

Now you're ready to transform your daily tasks into epic adventures with Questify. Happy questing!
